Classic Waffles

These waffles turn out lovely and crispy. Perfect for any day of the week! With just basic pantry staples like flour, eggs, and milk, these waffles require very little prep.

Home cooks of all levels praise this simple method for creating fluffy, crisp waffles every time. Allrecipes member Nikki Badzik shares, "They were crunchy on the outside, soft on the inside."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 4 teaspoons baking poweder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  4. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
  5. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
